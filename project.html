From Farm to Your Home

Amla Juice

Amla Juice from Parshv Agro is a refreshing daily drink made from carefully selected amla fruit, naturally rich in Vitamin C. Each batch is processed under hygienic conditions to preserve nutrition and flavor without relying on artificial colors.

  • Supports daily immunity and overall wellness
  • Balanced taste that can be enjoyed with water or other mixes
  • No artificial colors, made with care in Maharashtra
Amla Candy (Sweet & Chatpata)

Parshv Agro Amla Candy offers the goodness of amla in convenient, bite-sized pieces, available in both sweet and chatpata flavors. It is crafted to be a tasty and handy way to enjoy amla benefits during travel, at work, or as an after-meal delight.

  • Two flavor options: classic sweet and tangy chatpata
  • Ideal for kids and adults who prefer chewable formats
  • Packed for freshness and easy sharing
Amla Mawa

Amla Mawa is a rich, traditional-style confection that blends the tartness of amla with the creaminess of mawa. Created for those who enjoy classic sweets with a nutritional twist, it brings together flavor, indulgence, and the natural benefits of amla.

  • Suitable for festive occasions and special gifting
  • Combines traditional taste with the goodness of amla
  • Crafted in controlled batches for consistent quality
Kokam Syrup

Parshv Agro Kokam Syrup is a tangy-sweet concentrate crafted from kokam, known for its naturally cooling properties. Perfect for summer, it can be mixed with chilled water or soda to create refreshing beverages that help you beat the heat.

  • Ideal for preparing quick, cooling summer drinks
  • Naturally tangy flavor with balanced sweetness
  • Great for family gatherings and home refreshment counters
